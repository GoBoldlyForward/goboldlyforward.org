A note on donations

Thank you, but keep it.

Go Boldly Foundation is already fully funded through the quiet generosity of a small, private group of donors. We are not raising money right now, and honestly, we would rather your generosity went somewhere it is needed more.

No donation form here. On purpose.

If you came here to help

Here is where your support goes further.

Lead a nonprofit? Apply.

The best way to use our resources is to put them to work. Tell us about your mission and the opportunity in front of you.

Give to National Angels.

National Angels walks alongside foster children, families, and the caseworkers who serve them. If you want to give, send it straight to them.

Spread the word.

Know a nonprofit that is held back by its tools? Point them our way. A referral is worth more to us than a check.

Are you a funder who wants to help us build for more organizations? That is a different conversation, and a welcome one. Reach out at morgan@goboldlyforward.com.
