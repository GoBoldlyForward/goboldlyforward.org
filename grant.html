The Go Boldly Grant

$50,000 in custom software services for nonprofits ready to multiply their impact.

From the first conversation to the last line of code, we build with you, at no cost to your organization. One award. One full engagement. One mission, accelerated.

Start Your Application
What the grant is

A complete software engagement, fully funded.

The grant is an in-kind contribution: not a check, but a full software engagement delivered by our team at no cost to you. Each cycle, one nonprofit is awarded the complete build: strategy, design, development, and the first months of operating it in the wild.

Who it is for

  • A registered 501(c)(3) (or international equivalent) in good standing.
  • An active program serving real people today.
  • A specific opportunity that the right software could unlock.
  • 4-6 hours per week available to collaborate during the build.
PHASE 01

Discovery & Ideation

Workshops to clarify the problem, the audience, and the outcomes that matter most.

PHASE 02

Prototype & Plan

A working prototype and detailed blueprint, approved before we build.

PHASE 03

Production Build

Your tool, engineered to production standards and deployed for real use.

PHASE 04

Ongoing Operation

How it works

From application to launch, the path is clear.

Step 01

Apply

Tell us about your organization and the opportunity in front of you. The application takes about 30 minutes.

Step 02

Conversation

We follow up with finalists for a 45-minute conversation to understand fit on both sides.

Step 03

Award & Build

One organization is awarded each cycle. From there, we begin the full engagement together.
