The Go Boldly Grant

$50,000 in custom software services for nonprofits ready to multiply their impact.

From the first conversation to the last line of code, we build with you, at no cost to your organization. One award. One full engagement. One mission, accelerated.

What's included

A complete software engagement, fully funded.

The grant covers every phase of building and launching custom software for your organization: strategy, design, development, and the first months of operating it in the wild.

PHASE 01

Discovery & Ideation

Workshops to clarify the problem, the audience, and the outcomes that matter most.

PHASE 02

Prototype & Plan

A working prototype and a detailed blueprint, so you see and approve the full vision before we build.

PHASE 03

Production Build

Your tool, designed and engineered to production standards, deployed for real-world use.

PHASE 04

Ongoing Operation

Hands-on maintenance, oversight, and improvement during the critical first months in operation.

Who we're looking for

Is the grant a fit for your organization?

The grant is designed for established nonprofits with a clear mission, an active program, and a real opportunity that the right software could unlock. You don't need a technical co-founder. That's our role to play.

  • You're a registered 501(c)(3) (or international equivalent) in good standing.
  • You have an existing program serving real beneficiaries today.
  • You can identify a specific bottleneck, opportunity, or initiative that software could meaningfully change.
  • Your team has 4 to 6 hours per week available for collaboration during the engagement.
  • You're ready to operate and own the software once it launches.
