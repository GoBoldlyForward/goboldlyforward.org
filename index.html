How it works

A complete software engagement: strategy to launch.

Off-the-shelf tools never quite fit, and custom software feels out of reach. We close that gap with a repeatable, transparent process, fully funded by the grant.

Phase 01

Ideate

We clarify the problem worth solving and align on what to build, and why.

Phase 02

Prototype

A working blueprint you can see and approve before a line of code is written.

Phase 03

Build

Your blueprint becomes a real, deployed product your team and community can use.

Operate

We handle the first months of maintenance and iteration so the tool earns its keep.
